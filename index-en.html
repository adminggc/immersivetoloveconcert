THE ARTIST

Sir. Niels Lan Doky

Niels Lan Doky is a Danish-Vietnamese jazz pianist, composer, producer, film director, and author. Knighted by Queen Margrethe II of Denmark in 2010 for his outstanding contributions to music and culture, he serves as an official Danish cultural ambassador. Recognized as "The Best Pianist of His Generation" by Japan's prestigious Swing Journal, his remarkable career includes performing for Pope Jean Paul II at the Vatican and collaborating with jazz legends such as Niels-Henning Ørsted Pedersen, Jack DeJohnette, and John Scofield.

His illustrious career has graced the world's most prestigious stages, from Carnegie Hall (New York), Royal Albert Hall (London), to Blue Note (Tokyo). El Diario hails him as "one of the most important pianists of the past half-century," while The New York Times praises his work as "fresh and provocative." With 45 albums released since his 1986 debut, Niels Lan Doky continues to redefine the boundaries of contemporary jazz.

After nearly a decade, the Knight of Jazz returns to Vietnam on a journey to his roots. For this exclusive performance, he brings his trio featuring Felix Pastorius on guitar bass and Jonas Johansen on drums, promising an evening of world-class jazz that transcends boundaries and touches the soul.

SPECIAL COLLABORATION

Guest Artists

A special highlight is the collaboration between Sir Niels Lan Doky and Vietnam's leading artists such as People's Artist Thanh Lam, singer Hà Trần, and saxophonist Quyền Thiện Đắc – names with a clear mark in both preserving the beauty and identity of Vietnamese music, while exploring, creating and innovating to integrate into the global music flow. This convergence creates a multi-dimensional musical space, where the international Jazz spirit meets contemporary Vietnamese folk breath in a high-level musical dialogue, harmonious and full of improvisation.

Diva Thanh Lam is one of Vietnam's most celebrated vocalists, known for her powerful voice and emotional performances.
